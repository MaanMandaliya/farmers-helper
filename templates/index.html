Features


Species Recognition

There are hundreds of kinds of trees in the natural ecosystem, and it can be very difficult to distinguish between them. Botanists and those who study plants however, are able to identify the type of tree at a glance by using the characteristics of the leaf. Machine learning is used to automatically classify leaf types.

Users can easily get details of species by taking pictures of it and upload it here. The system will result with the name and details of the species.

Disease Detection

Crop diseases are a major threat to food security, but their rapid identification remains difficult in many parts of the world due to the lack of the necessary infrastructure. The combination of increasing global smartphone penetration and recent advances in computer vision made possible by deep learning has paved the way for disease diagnosis.

Every disease, pest and deficiency leaves behind a specific pattern. Farmer's Helper recognizes these patterns. One photo is enough and you know what your plant is missing.



Weed Classification

Our system can discriminate crop and weed plants growing in commercial fields where crop and weed grow close together and handles overlap between plants. Automated crop / weed discrimination enables weed control strategies with specific treatment of weeds to save cost and mitigate environmental impact.

Users can easily classify between weed by taking pictures of it and upload it here. The system will classify correctly with higher accuracy and provide a solution to get rid of it.

Crop Yield Prediction

This system introduce a scalable, accurate, and inexpensive method to predict crop yields using publicly available remote sensing data.

Remote sensing data are globally available and relatively inexpensive. It is frequently used in computational sustainability applications.
